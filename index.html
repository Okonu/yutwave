Who we are!

Following the urgent need to save the capsizing generational cruise, a group of young men and women coined an idea on how to make it possible to engage young persons from different socio-economic sectors to connect and live interdependently. To implement the idea, they came up with The Youth Wave.
The Youth Wave is an intellectual movement that was coined for the purpose of bringing youths together to network, share ideas, exchange knowledge and explore their potential. With recurrent editions of such events, we aim to reach out to as many youths as possible.
The Youth Wave seeks to provide a platform in which Synergies across different disciplines amongst the youth will be bolstered. With a generation of young and ambitious talent as a society we are tasked to work together for collective success

Our Mission

To provide a platform for youths to network and create synergies in their respective fields of interest. The Youth Wave.

The Pioneer Editions

The Pioneers Edition were a success being the launchpads of the Youth Wave. The events helped to share the spirit and thoughts of the founders and partners with every Youth through out the country.
Youths in the country have ambitions but lack of knowledge on how and where to grab the opportunites has left many of us in desperate situations.
The coming editions seek to create a platform where "Seekers" and "Finders" meet to work on given areas of mutual interst and in turn create a network.

Objectives

The aims of each of our events are:-
- Youths have made new connections.
- Youths have learned new ideas.
- Young entrepreneurs have exchanged ideas and skills.
- To have changed the mentality of failure and instilled the idea of self-drive.
- To have youths work together in different projects.
- Connect like minded youths.
